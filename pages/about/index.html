About

I’m a Ph.D. candidate with the Electronic Design Automation group, University of Kaiserslautern, Germany. My current research is focused on improving the precision and scalability of software analysis at binary level. To this end, I’m exploring several ideas that happen to be at the intersection of formal methods and applied engineering.

I welcome your comments on ammarbenkhadra@gmail.com. You can find more about me on home page Linkedin, and Github. You might also be interested in the atom feeds of this blog.
