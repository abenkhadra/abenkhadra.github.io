About

I am a researcher at Fraunhofer IESE where I conduct applied research and develop software for simulation and virtual hardware prototyping. My work lays at the intersection between software analysis, virtual hardware prototyping, and computer architecture. In this blog, I share various information that I find interesting along the way.
